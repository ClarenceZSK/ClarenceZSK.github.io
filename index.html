Shengkai Zhang

Ph.D. Candidate

Huazhong University of Science and Technology

Biography

Shengkai Zhang (张胜凯) will receive his Ph.D. degree from the EIC Department, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) this winter. His advisor is Prof. Tao Jiang. He received his M.Sc and M.Phil degrees from HUST and Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2012 and 2015, respectively.
